Information about other people that live in your home.

Ask the claimant for all the information you need about people that live in their home, including:

  • their full names
  • their National insurance number, or Child Benefit Number if they are under 16
  • their Dates of birth
  • whether they are registered blind or severely sight impaired
  • whether they claim DLA or PIP

If the person they live with is a boarder or lodger, ask:

  • how much they pay the claimant and how often
  • whether the claimant provides cooked meals for them
