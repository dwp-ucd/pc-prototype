Apply for Pension Credit

If you apply online, we will pay you into the account you get your State Pension paid into. You will be able to change this once you have started receiving your Pension Credit payments.

We will use information:

  • that we already have about your State Pension and any benefits you receive
  • that we get from HMRC about your income and earnings

To find out more about how we use information and your information rights, read our Personal Information Charter.

You will need to complete your application all in one go. You will not be able to save it and come back later.

To keep your information secure, if you do not enter any information for more than an hour, your application will time out and you'll have to start again.
