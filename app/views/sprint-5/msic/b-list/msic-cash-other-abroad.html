Money held in other countries

Money held in other countries is treat as capital of its type in the UK. For example, a Spanish ISA is the same as a UK ISA for PC purposes.

To record the amount we need to know the currency of the money. The exchange rate is worked using the XE currency converter

Any capital spent to transfer the money to a UK account is disregarded.

To record these accounts we would need:

  • The account type
  • Bank name and last 4 digits of the (foreign equivalent) account number.
  • Plus any additional details that the type of account requires in the spreadsheet. (Note for @Mark)
