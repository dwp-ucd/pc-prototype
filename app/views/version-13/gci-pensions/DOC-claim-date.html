Agent: Work out the earliest date a claim can be considered from

Talk to the claimant and use the information available in CAM and SearchLight to help them decide the best date to start their claim.

Things that could influence this date are:

  • previous claims for Pension Credit or other benefits
  • changes to whether they live with a partner or anyone else
  • changes to the amount of money they have, their income or employment status
  • whether they have spent longer than 4 weeks outside the UK since the earliest claim date, unless it was connected to medical treatment, convalescence or the death of a partner or child
