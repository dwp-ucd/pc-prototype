How we use personal information

We only use your information as allowed by data protection laws. This includes using information we already have and sharing information with other organisations and government departments where we need to.

For example, we will use information:

  • that we already have about your State Pension and any benefits you receive
  • that we get from HMRC about your income and earnings

You can get more information in our Personal Information Charter.
