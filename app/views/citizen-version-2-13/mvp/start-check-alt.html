Do you have the right information to make an application for Pension Credit?

We will ask for some information about you and your partner (if you have one) while you make your application.

Make sure you have this ready before starting your application.

You will not be able to save your application and come back later.

You will need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • bank statements, usually for the last three months
  • details of any income, pensions, savings or investments
