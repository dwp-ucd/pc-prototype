Partner details

If you were born on or before 5 October 1954 you could get between £100 and £300 to help you pay your heating bills. This is known as a Winter Fuel Payment.

Housing details

You can get a bus pass for free travel. You can get this when you reach State Pension age if you live in England, or when you reach age 60 if you live in Scotland or Wales.

Go to GOV.UK and search for "free bus pass".

Or call your local council as they issue the bus passes.

Housing costs
