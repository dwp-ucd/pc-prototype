What happens next

{% if data["home-care-home-check"] == "Yes" or data["money-second-property-check"] == "Yes" %}

We will send you a form by DD / Month / Year (4 weeks) to ask you for details of the property or land that you own.

{% else %}

We are doing some final checks on your application. We will contact you by DD / Month / Year (4 weeks) either to confirm your award or ask you for more information. You do not need to contact us before then unless anything changes.

{% endif %}

If your application is successful we will pay your Pension Credit into the same account as your State Pension.

You will be able to change this to a different account once your Pension Credit is in payment.

If anything changes

While you are receiving Pension Credit, you must tell us straight away about any changes that could affect the amount of Pension Credit you get (opens in a new window or tab).

Housing Benefit

If you pay rent for the place where you live, you might qualify for Housing Benefit. We will make this application for you using the details from your Pension Credit claim. Your local authority will contact you.

Council Tax Reduction

If your application is successful, you might be able to claim Council Tax Reduction. You should contact your local authority to apply.

Carer's Allowance

You might be able to claim Carer's Allowance (Opens in a new window or tab) if you care for someone for 35 hours or more a week.
