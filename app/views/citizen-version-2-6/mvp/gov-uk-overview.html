What Pension Credit is

Pension Credit makes sure people who are over State Pension age and on a low income have enough money for their living costs in retirement.

Pension Credit is separate from your State Pension.

You can get Pension Credit even if you have savings or own your own home. You might get extra help if you are a carer, severely disabled, or responsible for a child or young person.

If you get Pension Credit, you can also get help with:

  • housing costs, such as service charges and ground rent
  • the cost of NHS dental treatment, glasses and transport costs for hospital appointments
  • Cold Weather Payments

If you get Pension Credit, you can also apply for:

  • Council Tax Reduction
  • Housing Benefit if you rent the property you live in, or support with mortgage interest if you own it
  • a free TV licence if you are over 75
