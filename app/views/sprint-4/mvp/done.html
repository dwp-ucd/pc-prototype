What happens next

We are doing some final checks on your application. We will write to you by 15 January either to confirm your award or ask you for more information.

If anything changes

You should tell us straight away if anything changes that affects how much Pension Credit you are entitled to. This includes:

  • if you go abroad for longer than 4 weeks
  • your address changes, or anyone moves in or out of your home
  • your income changes, including any changes to employment or self employment
  • changes to the value of your savings, investments or property
  • if you go into hospital or a care home for more than 4 weeks
