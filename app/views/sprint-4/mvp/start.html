Apply for Pension Credit

Use this service to start a Pension Credit application

This part of your application should take around 20 minutes depending on your circumstances.

You will need to tell us about:

  • your National Insurance number
  • any children you look after
  • any benefits you get
  • any pensions you get
  • any money, savings or investments you have, including balances, account and reference numbers
  • any second properties or land you own
  • earnings from employment and any other income. You can find this on your P45, P60 or any pay slips
  • details of the bank account you want any Pension Credit paid into

Personal information

We will treat your information carefully and only use it as allowed by data protection laws.

To find out more about how we use information and your information rights, read our Personal Information Charter. You can get a copy of this at a jobcentre.
