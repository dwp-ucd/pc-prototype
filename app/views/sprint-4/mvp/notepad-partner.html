Information about the claimant's partner.

Ask the claimant for all the information you need, including:

  • their partner's name
  • their partner's date of birth
  • their partner's Nationality
  • their partner's National Insurance number
  • whether their partner has agreed to this application
  • whether their partner is registered blind and the date they were registered

If their partner was sponsored to come to the UK in last 5 years ask for:

  • the start and end dates of any periods their partner has spent abroad in the last 5 years
  • their partner's reason for leaving the UK
  • whether their partner's sponsor is still alive
