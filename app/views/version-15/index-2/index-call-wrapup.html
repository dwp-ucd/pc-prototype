Application in progress

What happens next

We are doing some final checks on your application. We will contact you by DD / Month / Year (6 - 8 weeks) either to confirm your award or ask you for more information. You do not need to contact us before then unless anything changes.

If anything changes

While you are receiving Pension Credit, you must tell us straight away about any changes that could affect the amount of Pension Credit you get.

If you do not report any changes, we might reduce or stop your Pension Credit.

Changes that could affect your claim include:

  • if you go abroad for longer than 4 weeks
  • your address changes, or anyone moves in or out of your home
  • your income changes, including any changes to employment or self-employment
  • changes to the value of your savings, investments or second properties
  • if you go into hospital or a care home for more than 4 weeks

Contact the Pension Service helpline to report any changes:

  • Telephone: 0800 731 0469
  • Textphone: 0800 169 0133
  • Next Generation Text (NGT) text relay (if you cannot hear or speak on the phone): 18001 then 0800 731 0469
  • Monday to Friday, 8am to 7:30pm

  • Find out about call charges (opens in a new window or tab)

You can also report by post. The address is on the letters you get about your Pension Credit.
