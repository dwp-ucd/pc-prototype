You must have the right information ready

You need to make sure you have certain information to hand while you make your application online.

You will need this information for you and your partner (if you live with one).

What to do if you do not have a National Insurance number

You can still apply for Pension Credit by phone or post if you do not have a National Insurance number.

Apply by phone

A friend or family member can call for you if you cannot use the phone.

It is taking longer than usual to apply by phone because of coronavirus (COVID-19).

Pension Credit claim line

Telephone: 0800 99 1234

Textphone: 0800 169 0133

NGT text relay (if you cannot hear or speak on the phone):
18001 then 0800 99 1234

Monday to Friday, 9.30am to 3:30pm
Find out about call charges

Apply by post

To apply by post, print out and fill in the Pension Credit claim form or call the claim line to ask for a form.

Send the claim form to the Pension Service, or ask someone to do it for you.

Contact a voluntary organisation such as Citizens Advice or Age UK if you need help with the form.
