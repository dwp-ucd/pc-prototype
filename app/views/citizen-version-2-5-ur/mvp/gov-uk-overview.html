What Pension Credit is

Pension Credit makes sure people who are over State Pension age and on a low income have enough money to live on in retirement.

Pension Credit tops up your weekly income if it’s below £173.75 if you live alone or £265.20 if you live with a partner.

Pension Credit is paid into an account and you do not pay tax on it.

As well as this payment, if you are eligible for Pension Credit, you might also be eligible for:

  • Council Tax Reduction
  • Housing Benefit, or help with mortgage interest, service charges and ground rent
  • free NHS dental treatment, help with the cost of glasses and transport costs to hospital appointments
  • a free TV license if you are over 75
  • Cold Weather Payment
This guide is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
