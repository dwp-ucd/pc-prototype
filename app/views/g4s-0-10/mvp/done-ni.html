You will need to contact the Northern Ireland Pension Centre

You can check your eligibility and apply for Pension Credit online by visiting nidirect.gov.uk and searching for Pension Credit. It is quick, easy and secure.

If you cannot go online, you can contact the Pension Centre by phone.

  • Telephone: 0808 100 6165
  • Textphone (freephone) (for deaf or hard of hearing users and customers with speech difficulties): 0808 100 2198

Monday to Friday, 9am to 4pm (closed on bank holidays).
Contacting 08 and 03 numbers

If you need to email the Northern Ireland Pension centre: pensionservice@dfcni.gov.uk
