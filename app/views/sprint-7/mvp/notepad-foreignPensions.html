Information about foreign Pensions

Have a conversation with the claimant to:

  • tell the claimant that any notional income will affect their eligibility for and entitlement to Pension Credit
  • ask for documents from the claimant or a conference call with the pension provider to show the maximum annual pension payable

If documents are not available and the information cannot be gathered conference call:

  • send form PPR1 for each pension scheme that may be potentially classed as a notional income
  • set a 6 week task from the date form PPR1 is issued
  • take notional income into consideration when the task matures, or when the PPR1 has been returned
  • ask an appropriate person to make a notional income decision where needed

Scheme Details

  • Who is the scheme with?
  • Date of entitlement?
  • Lumpsum or regular payments
