You must have the right information ready

If you do not enter anything for more than 30 minutes, your application will time out and you will have to start again. This is to keep your details secure.

So you need to make sure you have certain information to hand while you make your application.

You will need this information for you and your partner (if you have one).

The information you will need:

  • National Insurance number
  • bank statements, usually for the last three months
  • details of any income, pensions, savings or investments
